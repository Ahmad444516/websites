    • About Dawat-e-Islami

    Dawat-e-Islami, a global non-political movement for the propagation of the Holy Quran and Sunnah, is serving Ummah with its Islamic centres in 195 countries of the world. Different departments are being set up to execute the Madani activities of spreading the teachings of Quran and Sunnah effectively. Dawat-e-Islami was founded by Shaykh-e-Tariqat, Ameer-e-Ahl-e-Sunnat Hazrat Allamah Maulana Abu Bilal Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadiri Razavi in 1981.

    Intro of Ameer E Ahlesunnat

    The Spiritual guide, Shaykh of Tariqah, Ameer of Ahl Al-Sunnah, the Honorable ‘Allama Maulana Abu Bilal Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri Razavi دَامَـتْ بَـرَكَـاتُـهُـمُ الْـعَـالِـيَـهْ was born on the 26th of Ramadan, 1369 A.H. (12th July 1950) in Karachi, Pakistan. His father Haji Abdur Rahman Qadiri رَحْمَةُ اللهِ تَعَالٰی عَلَيْه was steadfast on the Islamic guidelines and was a righteous individual, overall. He would often walk with a lowered gaze, and knew many Ahadith by heart. He never had a rapacious desire to gather the fleeting materialistic things of this world. He was a disciple in the esteemed Qadiriyyah Sufi order. Inspiring as a spiritual guide and Supporting Community Ameer of Ahl al-Sunnah دَامَـتْ بَـرَكَـاتُـهُـمُ الْـعَـالِـيَـهْ is the Khalifah [i.e. successor].of Grand Mufti of Pakistan, Hazrat Mufti Muhammad Waqaruddin Qadiri Razavi رَحْمَةُ اللهِ تَعَالٰی عَلَيْه . His sincere, tireless and determined efforts for preaching and practicing Sunnah have guided millions of sinful people to the path of piety and righteousness. And thus, by the grace of Allah, this movement has now spread in over 175 countries.
